Volunteer Opportunities

All passionate and dedicated animal lovers are welcomed! Join our team to make a pawsitive difference in your community.


In the embrace of an animal shelter, I found my purpose and witnessed the resilience of the forgotten souls.

Specialized Volunteers

Contribute with specific skills such as:

  • Photography
  • Graphic Design
  • Web Design
  • Accountants
  • Media specialists
  • Dog Trainer
Animal Care Volunteers

Assist in daily care of the animals with tasks such as:

  • Clean & up-keep shelter
  • Feed & give water
  • Bath animals
  • Provide basic grooming
  • Media specialists
  • Socialize animals
Medical Volunteers

Volunteers with veterinary experience such as:

  • Spay-neuter
  • Vaccine administering
  • Animal behaviorists
  • Policy development

Join our team!

If you are interested in volunteering, please contact us by completing our form. Kindly be aware that all volunteers are expected to commit to a minimum of two hours of volunteer work.
